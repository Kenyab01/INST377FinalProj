About TerraBus

TerraBus is a project designed to enhance the public transit experience in Washington D.C. by providing real-time bus and metro updates. Our platform ensures commuters, tourists, and residents have access to accurate and timely information for a smoother journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the commuting experience by offering reliable and user-friendly access to public transit data. We aim to reduce travel uncertainties and make public transportation a more attractive and efficient choice.

Features

Our platform offers real-time bus tracking and delay notifications, interactive maps with nearby stops and routes, and historical data insights to enhance travel planning.
